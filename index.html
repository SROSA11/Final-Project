About Me

I'm Steve, I have a positive attitude and a passion for things that make me think. I am currently the Sales Operations Manager in the Atlanta region. I have been with the company for 9 years, starting with the Ford Credit Leadership Development Program in the Nashville Business Center! I have had the opportunity to work in Customer Service, Origination's, Dealer Credit, and also as a Team Leader. My various roles have provided a strong background in understanding our business operations. I continue to look for ways to improve on our processes as well as improving myself. I find learning new skills and challenging myself to be rewarding. I'm gritty and I believe in my abilities and competencies to understand complex subjects. I graduated from Clemson University earning a B.S. in Financial Management, with an emphasis on portfolio management. In my free time I enjoy researching and investing. I enjoy mentally stimulating activities and believe pursuing a new skillset is an exciting opportunity. Additionally, I have the mental toughness to persevere and remain optimistic, along with humility to ask for help and seek guidance when needed.

I want to be a software engineer because I see the endless possibilities to create new and exciting products. I see it as a vehicle for the future and the future for our vehicles. I enjoy the process of problem solving and building tools in my current role, and look to apply those skills to software engineering. As a perpetual learner, the "Learn to Code" program is a unique opportunity to build a new set of skills while continuing to learn and develop within the company. I believe my knowledge and background of sales and finance with additional skills in programming will lead to innovative changes for Ford Credit.
