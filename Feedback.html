Feedback!

Everyone loves a bit of constructive criticism! Once the “Profile” page and the “Recipe” pages were created I decided to share the website for feedback to make revisions. I went through several iterations of the website and worked diligently to make the site complete before sharing. I was satisfied with what I created but thought it could use some improvements. After emailing it out to friends, family, and peers I was provided with a ton of suggestions!

On the Recipe page, the sizing and spacing of the pictures needed more consistency and definition. Also, the instructions made sense to me, but would they make sense to everyone? I sought out my friend's mom for advice, she is a Speech-Language Pathologist and went through my site with a fine tooth comb to ensure grammatical accuracy! My sister, who helped me cook the gnocchi, reviewed the instructions just as meticulously.

Additionally, while taking a look on her lunch break at work, my Mom shared it with the all the nurses in her OBGYN unit to provide suggestions! The more the merrier!

I also sent the website to my boss for feedback. She is a great advocate and strategic thinker who helped me to see it from a new perspective. My friends and family know my passion for cooking and creating difficult yet delicious meals, so making a recipe was natural. The profile page was more professional and didn't mention cooking in the first drafts. I was encouraged to share more about me personally to help connect the dots and make the site more cohesive.

Feel free to let me know what you would improve!
